Music as Metaphor

kamagapÄ“ uses music as a metaphor for physical intimacy. People have been making music and making whoopee since time began, so it's a natural fit.

Measure

A touch. Every touch is an action in time (how long), performed with a tempo (how fast), and a dynamic (how intense). A hug at the end of the day is long, slow, and deep. A kiss on the way out the door is short, quick, and light.

Score

A sequence of Measures. Every touch happens in context, as part of a whole experience. A back massage isn't a single kind of touch, but a variety of techniques with different timing and intensity, performed in a certain order.

How To

1. Find a Score. You and your partner look through the new Scores, search by theme, or pull up one of your favorites. Find one that works for you both, based on what you're in the mood for, and how much time/energy you have.

2. Play a Score. Each measure of the Score will be shown on the screen. You and your partner play along with the Score by performing the actions described in each measure.

3. Talk about it. After the Score, talk about it. What was your favorite part? What part could have been skipped? (If you're playing a score that is new, be sure to check in with each other during the Score.)
